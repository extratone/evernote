The Battle of Athens Reenactment will feature vendors selling food and period items and tours of the Cannonball House beginning at 9 a.m. at Battle of Athens State Historic Site.

At 1 p.m. both days, a re-enactment to commemorate Missouri's northernmost Civil War battle will take place on the battlefield.

Reenactors will be from the Missouri Brigade Medical Service. For more information about this event, call 660-877-3871.
