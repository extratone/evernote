ONE BLOCK


One Block presents some of the greatest images from Luvelli, one of the world’s most unique and outstanding street photographers. This collectors publication displays Luvelli’s power to capture the essence of the Midwestern United States unlike any artist has done before.

Street photography in it’s best form, can radiate human emotions and enhance human spirit. In the Midwest Missouri town of Columbia, there’s been a long history of eclectic characters and hidden beauty that can easily be missed by the average patron. After spending three years capturing every aspect and walk of life that inhabits Ninth Street, Luvelli decided to produce the photography book “One Block”, to share his perspective of an area that has made an impact on his life emotionally and professionally.

Emotion and the non-stop interactions around us first needs to be comprehensible in order to be appreciated and Luvelli’s photography is the most powerful medium to achieve this.
