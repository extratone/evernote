You may have been aware that The Movie Closet's creator, Ben Stokes, is an artist, by trade.
 
This summer, he'll be illustrating a bi-weekly webcomic with words by Bryan Nelson, called
Harvey T. Halloway. 
 
Harvey is an insectoid-humanoid detective, and his neo-noir, black and white world is not in poverty of detail, by any means.
 
Be sure to bookmark Harvey's Section, take a gander at pages One and Two, and subscribe to Spanktravision on YouTube for a behind-the-scenes look at the comic's creation.
