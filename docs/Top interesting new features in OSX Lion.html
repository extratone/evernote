Top interesting new features in OSX Lion

Created:7/23/2011 6:48 PM
Updated:7/23/2011 7:35 PM

  • High-resolution cursor
"In Lion, the cursor is crisp and sharp at larger sizes."

  • Gesture navigation in Finder
  • "Group as folder" function
  • Full screen applications - may seem insignificant, but I used Safari in full screen on a 27" iMac today, and it was a great (but impractical) experience.
  • Day and year view in iCal
  • Everything about the new Mail
  • Gesture navigation in Misson Control
  • In general, the new multi touch gestures are genius.  Especially spreading three fingers and your thumb to "show desktop."
  • Adding your signature to documents in Preview
Quicktime Player
  • Merge clips
  • Export audio only
  • Rotate clips
  • Capture a specific region of the screen
  • Show clicks on capture (Basically replacing Screenflow at this point.)
Safari
  • Full screen (absolutely brilliant.)
  • Tap to zoom
  • Increased HTML5 performance
  • Drag and drop from the downloads folder!  HOLY SHIT!
  • Reading list (basically a queue of webpages to read.)

"Per-user screen sharing

You can remotely log in to a Mac with any user account on that computer and control it, without interrupting someone else who might be using the computer under a different login."
"

Observe Only mode


The new Observe Only mode lets you watch a remote computer without controlling the mouse or trackpad movements."

Spotlight
  • Drag and drop from the spotlight menu. 
  • Quick look preview in spotlight.
  • Searches search engines.  
  • Searches Wikipedia for definition.
"

Lookup


Double-tap with three fingers to select a word or phrase in any OS X application and look it up. OS X gathers information from the built-in dictionary and thesaurus, and goes to the web to bring back related Wikipedia entries. Results are displayed inline under the selected word."

Text
"

Character picker


Accessing accented characters in OS X Lion is a breeze. Just hold down the letter and a dialog appears listing all alternative and accented characters."


Improved auto-correction

Auto-correction in Lion displays suggested spellings below the word. Press Return to accept the change or click the X to keep the current spelling.

Dictionary application

The Dictionary application in Lion displays the definition of a word as you type, giving you instant results.

Resize from any edge

You can now resize a window from any side or corner.

Interact with iTunes artwork in screen saver

Turn your screen saver into a jukebox. Just click an album to play the songs.
