The theme of this update is fresh start and rebirth.
From The South To The North.


So what a couple of weeks it has been. I never thought that just a month after deploying from the south for this war that I would be writing to all of you with not only us having booted goons and its allies out of deklein and branch but on top of that we would be the ones settling it. The work done over the past 5 weeks from both members of guardians and other alliances from the greater moneybadger coalition has been amazing. For all the bullshit spun out from our enemies about the hatred and dissent inside the moneybadger leadership and fcs I have been amazingly surprised that the war has been smooth and nearly drama free throughout.

The Future.
From the Guardians side of things. I would just like to say from all of us in leadership that you guys have done an amazing job with the grind be it the entosiser bros or the fcs staying up hours on end you all did an amazing job. We took ourselves right to the edge of burnout and have come back with a slight tan. We are now on the other side of the main grind and find ourselves with a little more freedom to help out the other members of moneybadgers as and when they need them. With this in mind we told all of your leadership to give members a break from the grind this week and use the time to prepare for what comes next. Alongside with this, on sunday night we will be reseting all the entities we had down south still blue. This is to help clear up our list with just who we are working with up north and any new blues we have collected along the way.

This saturday we will come back from our break and be moving to the staging system we will be using for the foreseeable future. We will use it as our base of operations for all ops from now on. We know that before this we have been a little fluid with regards to staging systems. Unless something changes then the system we will be moving to will be our system for now. This is not to say you are to move everything to there just that this will be where your doctrine ships will need to be. Which doctrines these are I will address here in a bit.

Teamspeak
We will be making another change upon return to regular campaigning regarding our combat operations. From Saturday onwards all fleets from roaming to CTAs will all be on darkness TS not on serperate comms of alliances. This is to easier secure and help people to work together in the future. It is to help with our internal communication and combat operations. Alongside this you need to make sure you are registered for the backup teamspeak server too. 185.2.169.44:1337 is the server. Get her registered and be ready to use it from next week incase the other one is hit by a ddos or something else happens.

Citadel and what it means for us.
The reason I waited till today to do this update was in part to laziness and to also give us a fresh start upon logging in after the patch. Not much will changing for us after the patch, we will for sure be putting down a citadel to stage from. However this will most likely only be a large citadel and not a XL for now. With regards to capitals. Dreads and titans and supers are the new big thing. We will need to get used to them again as its a complete relearn both at a line member and FC level. The new Faux carriers will make things interesting for combat. Fits and setups will be posted here this week or the next to incorporate the final builds that make it to tranquillity.

Doctrines.
With us finally being able to unify our doctrines from both doctrines we left in the south and ones we took on our deployment I will now list the doctrines we will be using as our main Offense/Defence doctrines for the north. These should be trained in order of listing.

Doctrine number 1: Proteus/legion fleet. This doctrine you need to have north and ready to move and use from this saturday.

Doctrine number 2: Cerberus Fleet. This doctrine has been our main northern doctrine up to now we will be continuing with these doctrine going forward so make sure you have it.

Doctrine number 3: Maelstrom fleet. Our main battleship doctrine for now, not as commonly used but needed nonetheless.

Doctrine number 4: Shield Tech 3 Destroyer fleet. This is our fast moving attack doctrine. Used by both main fcs and skirmish fcs.

Capitals: These are as usual. We will see how the meta plays out to how we do capitals going forward.

But what about the war Sort?
The war still rages on guys. The goons are still staging out of lowsec and still throwing fleets out at us time and time again. We will be staging closer soon to get in on some of the extra fights to be had. Our neighbours in fade are being trolled with by the goons and we simply cannot let that happen. We will work on helping them to stabilise and push out the goons while taking back any sov the goons have taken.

What do you need from me Sort?
The simple thing is guys keep doing what you have been doing for me this whole campaign so far. Keep fleeting up, keep supporting our fcs. Make sure you have doctrine ships ready. At your alliance level you should have plans for keeping the ADMs up. I expect at some point in the future we will have to defend. With this in mind we need to make sure we are ready for this.

To close this off I just want to say again. Thank you again for your service and dedication throughout this war. It is far from over but we are getting there guys. It is a pleasure to lead you guys. See you after the patch!

Let me know what you think about the current war and any questions you have as a reply below.
