    Just wanted to share some pictures of my newly acquired 1979 RX-7 GS in Spark Yellow
















    The dash is actually quite flawless under that stupid carpet cover but I'm gonna leave the carpet there unless I take it to a meet/show.



















    Oh, what is this? A rare wheel that I have 5 of? I think so :P



    Overall I am very satisfied with the car so far, the interior is perfect and the '84 seats are really nice. The whole under body and engine bay is coated so it won't have any rust problems down the road. Yes, there are things that need fixed (one of the rear drums are dragging) but he gave me so many extra parts I don't even think I have to buy anything to fix the few small problems it does have I couldn't be happier with anything else!
