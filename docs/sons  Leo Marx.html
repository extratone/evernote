        sons

        •February 10, 2017

        I have come from a line of Great men
        and lesser men
        men who lead proud and fulfilling lives
        and those who died before they had time
        these men worked with their hands
        and with their minds
        strong men and smart men
        locksmiths and welders,
        engineers and miners
        soldiers and sailors
        grunts to commanders
        their blood flows through me
        They were sons of America
        She brought them prosperity and disparity
        they loved her and hated her
        so when i die
        i will be buried in her heart land
        next to her mightiest river.
